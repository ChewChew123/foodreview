About Us

A student-built platform to celebrate Malaysia’s flavors — discover iconic dishes, hidden gems, and honest reviews.

Our Mission

To connect food lovers with authentic Malaysian street food — from timeless classics to neighborhood favorites — through a friendly, reliable guide.

  • Highlight real stalls, real dishes, and real reviews
  • Make it easy to filter by region, type, and rating
  • Promote local vendors and culture responsibly

Our Story

Street Food MY began as a student project driven by our love for Malaysian cuisine. We saw how hard it can be to find trustworthy local recommendations — so we created a guide that puts the dish first: what to eat, where to find it, and why it’s loved.

What We Value

Authenticity

Highlight dishes and stalls Malaysians truly love — no fluff, just flavor.

Clarity

Simple info, useful categories, and filters that actually help you decide.

Community

Respectful reviews and contributions that support small vendors nationwide.
